Collective MD (coMD)

coMD is a hybrid method with a VMD plugin designed for efficient simulations of conformational changes. ENM modes selected by a Monte Carlo/Metropolis algorithm are used for accelerating the MD trajectories (Gur et. al, 2013) CoMD can be used for estimating the conformational energy landscape (Gur et. al, 2015).

Features

coMD can be used for

  • Sampling the conformational space near native state and substates.
  • Generating transition paths between known conformers.
  • Obtaining information on global dynamics at atomic resolution.
  • Generating energy landscapes in the space of principal coordinates.
